Oure Best Selling Cat

Like Domestic Shorthair cats, Domestic Longhair cats have long hair and are of unknown ancestry. They’re especially common for this reason—most cats aren’t purebreds! Also, like their shorthaired friends, they were brought to North America from Europe via ships. These cats come in a full spectrum of colors, from orange to white and black to grey. Their coat is typically a few inches long, and they have distinct tufts around their ears and on the bottoms of their paws. Domestic Longhair cats benefit from regular grooming to help maintain their lovely coats. Their exact history is a mystery, but some speculate the recessive gene for long hair was pruned specifically by people with cats in colder climates like Russia, Iran, and Turkey.
